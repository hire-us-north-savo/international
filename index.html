WHY SHOULD YOU HIRE US?

What are the advantages of hiring international students?

They

are fluent in their native language

have a unique and comprehensive knowledge of their country's culture

study in a multicultural environment in English, which for most is not a mother tongue

have many skills and are studying to acquire even more

By being able to study in a foreign environment, international students have already proven that they are not afraid of challenges. Coming to Finland is not an easy process and those people already showed that they are determined.

Studying in an international environment is a unique and useful experience because we already know how to interact and communicate with individuals from various cultures.

Having received world-class education here in Finland, we are getting the same education as the locals. The skill sets are built here in Finland but at the same time, we already have skills developed from our home country. So adding them up together, we’re getting more experience which we can showcase to the companies.

HOW CAN THESE ISSUES BE SOLVED?

Integration into a company

We have several ideas for what companies could do to easily integrate the international workers into the existing team and simultaneously improve the trust and wellbeing in the current team.
A common lunch day when all the employees have their lunch together could be organized. That would give the international employees a chance to talk to their Finnish colleagues and to get to know each other.
It is commonly cited that there is often a lack of trust and openness in the Finnish work culture. During their free time, employees could play various games together including the games aimed at trust building. This would not only help the international hires to meet new Finnish friends among coworkers but it would also help the Finns trust each other more.
Many Finnish companies already have a so-called wellness day, or tyky päivä. It could be used as another way for the international talents to integrate into the company and to build trust among the employees.
Language and culture exchange. Employees of different nationalities can try teaching each other their native languages. For example, an employee from Russia could teach a Finnish coworker some Russian language, and in return the Finn would help the international employee learn Finnish.
Also, cultural exchange could be useful for team building. For example, during Christmas employees from different countries could share their unique traditional Christmas dishes.

Integration into the Finnish society

TE Toimisto

TE Office offers integration services for immigrants who have been in Finland for less than 3 years. This includes language courses and a personal integration plan for the individual. They also offer some extra training and internships to immigrants. Also, working immigrant can join their uneployment fund.

Navigaattori

Navigaattori is a place where Kuopio residents get expert help in finding suitable training and career options. Under the same roof various guidance and counselling services can be found.

Kotoutuminen.fi web service

The website is maintained by the Centre of Expertise in Immigrant Integration at the Ministry of Economic Affairs and Employment.

City of Joensuu

They provide integration services for people from abroad in different languages as well as services for City Counsil's employees and other partners relating to immigration and integration matters.

Language

A multitude of services for learning Finnish language exists in the North Savo region. Examples are listed below:

Kompassi

Kompassi offers Finnish language classes and courses, counselling for immigrants, help with hobbies and activities, and volunteer work.

Kuopion kansalaisopisto

Kuopio community college offers many courses in Finnish, Swedish, and other languages. They also offer many other courses, which may be useful for gaining some useful skills and also as hobbies.

TE Toimisto

As part of their integration plan, TE Office offers intensive courses in Finnish language to some individuals.
